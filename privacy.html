1. Privacy Policy

Our Philosophy: Privacy by Design

a Pea is built on a "Privacy-First" foundation. We believe your tasks, thoughts, and focus habits are sacred. We do not want, and technically cannot access, your personal information.

Data Storage & Collection

  • Local-Only Storage: All task content, "Pea" progress, and focus logs are stored strictly on your local device.
  • No Cloud Databases: We do not use any cloud services (such as Firebase or Supabase) to sync, store, or backup your personal data.
  • No Accounts: You can use all features of the app without registration. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

Diagnostics & Analytics

To maintain a high-fidelity "1/1000" user experience and ensure the app remains stable across various hardware, we use two privacy-conscious third-party services:

  • Sentry (Stability): Used to capture anonymous technical crash reports to fix bugs quickly.
  • TelemetryDeck (Usage Patterns): Collects fully anonymous usage patterns. It does not collect IP addresses or any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), and we do not use any cross-app tracking.
